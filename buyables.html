We Offer Variety Of Services; All To Satisfy You

KleanCycle.Ng Co is a leading service provider in the field of sustainable waste management and cleaning services. Our company is committed to providing a wide range of services that are aimed at keeping the environment clean and healthy. We offer recycling services to our clients to help reduce waste and promote sustainability. Our cleaning services are top-notch, using the latest equipment and eco-friendly products. We also sell cleaning products, including detergents, disinfectants, and other cleaning supplies. Our online services make it easy for our clients to access our services from the comfort of their homes. In addition to our core services, we also offer sales of footwear and clothing. Lastly, we offer web development services to help businesses create a strong online presence.At KleanCycle.Ng Co, we are committed to providing excellent customer service and helping our clients achieve their sustainability goals.

Plastic Bottles

Our buyback program at KleanCycle.Ng Co. offers a sustainable solution for plastic waste by purchasing used plastic bottles from individuals and businesses. By participating in this program, you can help divert plastic waste from landfills and support the circular economy. Join us at KleanCycle.Ng Co. in our efforts to reduce environmental impact and promote a greener future.

Cartons

KleanCycle.Ng is excited to offer our buyable service for your cartons! We specialize in purchasing cartons of all types and sizes, providing a convenient and eco-friendly solution to your excess inventory. Our streamlined process ensures you receive a fair price for your cartons, while helping to reduce waste. Contact us today to sell your cartons!

Plastic Bags & Wrappers

KleanCycle.Ng Co's service involves purchasing plastic bags and wrappers directly from customers to prevent them from being disposed of as waste. This initiative helps to minimize plastic pollution and encourages responsible waste management practices.

Tins

KleanCycle.Ng Co offers a convenient and eco-friendly way to dispose of tin cans by purchasing them directly from customers. This initiative helps reduce waste and promotes sustainable recycling practices. Choose KleanCycle.Ng Co and be a part of the solution to create a cleaner and healthier environment.
